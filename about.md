  <h1 style="margin-top: 0;">Jaehyoung Jeong</h1>

  <p>I am a Ph.D. candidate at <strong><a href="https://irrlab.github.io">IRRLAB</a></strong>, GIST. My research focuses on building the <strong>mathematical foundations of machine learning</strong>, specifically exploring <strong>Representation Learning</strong> and <strong>Geometric Deep Learning</strong>.</p>

    <li>Integrating <strong>ODE</strong> and <strong>Differential Geometry</strong> into generative frameworks.</li>
    <li>Explainable AI through <strong>Singular Learning Theory</strong>.</li>
  </ul>
